# Audit Workflow Research: Traditional & AI-Implemented at PwC, EY, and Deloitte

> **Scope:** Workpaper preparation, document vouching, IFRS/compliance tracking, and audit data collection
> **Firms surveyed:** PwC, EY (Ernst & Young), Deloitte
> **As of:** 25 June 2026
> **Evidence score:** High for Big 4 internal platforms and press releases; Moderate for third-party tool comparisons.

---

## 1. Traditional Workflows by Firm

### 1.1 PwC — Traditional Audit Workflow

**Core platforms:**
- **Aura** — engagement management, workpaper repository, file review
- **Halo** — risk assessment and analytics
- **Connect** — secure client portal for data collection
- **Viewpoint** — accounting and auditing research guidance

**Traditional step-by-step workflow:**

| Step | Activity | Manual Artifact |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Engagement acceptance & planning | Risk assessment in Halo; planning memos drafted in Word |
| 2 | Client data collection | Connect portal for PBC list; manual download of trial balance, invoices, contracts |
| 3 | Document vouching | PDF-to-Excel cross-referencing; staff print, highlight, tick-and-tie manually |
| 4 | Substantive testing | Workpapers in Aura; sampling done in Excel/IDEA; findings documented in narrative |
| 5 | IFRS/compliance tracking | Viewpoint for standards research; manual change-tracking spreadsheets |
| 6 | Review and sign-off | Partner review on printed/PDF workpapers; email threads for queries |
| 7 | Report issuance | Audit opinion drafted in Word; management letter assembled manually |

**Key pain points:**
- High admin burden during busy season (text matches MRI state)
- Manual change tracking for IFRS updates
- Document vouching at scale = thousands of PDF pages per engagement
- No automated anomaly detection

**Sources**
- PwC Transparency/Governance reports
- PwC audit methodology documentation
- PwC Indonesia service pages (regional reference)

**Confidence / Evidence Score: Medium-High**
- Internal platform names (Aura, Halo, Connect, Viewpoint) confirmed from global PwC documentation; specific Indonesia deployment details are inferred.

## 2. AI-Implemented Workflow by Firm

### 2.1 PwC — AI-Enabled Audit Workflow

**AI Platforms and Tools:**
- **ChatPwC / ChatNational** — internal GenAI assistant for research and drafting
- **Data PRO / Acquisition Hub (2025)** — AI-enabled tools for data collection and preparation; prebuilt/custom agents for critical workflows
- **PwC Agent OS** — operating system for deploying AI agents across workflows
- **Document Extraction API** — structured data extraction from complex multi-sheet spreadsheets and regulated documents
- **Digital Workmate** — AI document extraction combining rule-based ontologies with RAG (Llama-based)
- **250+ AI agents** deployed across PwC globally

**AI-implemented step-by-step workflow:**

| Step | Activity | AI Tool/Platform | Workflow Change |
|------|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Engagement planning | Aura next-gen + AI risk benchmarking | AI analyzes prior-year data and client risk profile; auto-suggest risk areas |
| 2 | Client data collection | Data PRO + Acquisition Hub | Prebuilt/custom agents execute PBC workflows; clients upload via intelligent portal |
| 3 | Document processing | Digital Workmate + Document Extraction API | OCR + NLP extracts structured data from invoices, contracts, spreadsheets — manual correction reduced |
| 4 | Workpaper preparation | Aura next-gen + AI | AI auto-populates workpapers from trial balance; auto-generates testing summaries |
| 5 | Substantive testing | AI-assisted sampling + anomaly detection | AI identifies high-risk items; auditor focuses on exceptions |
| 6 | IFRS/compliance tracking | Viewpoint + AI + ChatPwC | Automated monitoring of IFRS changes; impact flagged automatically; ChatPwC answers research queries |
| 7 | Review | AI draft review + Agent OS | AI pre-drafts findings and conclusions; partner reviews AI output rather than raw documents |
| 8 | Report issuance | GenAI drafting | AI generates first drafts of audit opinion, management letter, and communications |

**Key metrics and claims:**
- "Data is extracted, standardized, and transformed more consistently, helping prepare it for analysis without repetitive manual effort"
- PwC expects end-to-end AI audit automation within 2026
- 250+ AI agents deployed across the firm

**Sources**

### 2.2 EY Indonesia — AI-Implemented Audit Workflow (April 2026)

**AI Platforms and Tools:**
- **EY.ai** — unifying AI platform with EYQ (proprietary LLM)
- **EY Canvas** — global online audit platform augmented with **enterprise-scale agentic AI**
- **EY Helix** — analytics platform integrated with agentic AI
- **EY Atlas** — accounting guidance with AI augmentation
- **EYQ** — LLM for natural language queries
- **EY Intelligent Checklist** — AI-enhanced audit checklist
- Built on: **Microsoft Azure, Microsoft Foundry, Microsoft Fabric**

**Deployment scale (as of April 2026):**
- Enterprise-scale rollout across 150+ countries
- >160,000 engagements covered
- >2,000 EY Indonesia Assurance professionals enabled
- Processes >1.4 trillion journal entries

**AI-implemented step-by-step workflow:**

| Step | Activity | AI Tool/Platform | Workflow Change |
|------|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Engagement planning | EY Canvas + AI risk benchmarking | AI analyzes client risk profile; auto-generates audit plan and team allocation |
| 2 | Client data collection | EY Canvas unified interface | Automated data ingestion; AI validates completeness and format |
| 3 | Document processing | Multi-agent AI suite | Agents handle evidence collection, document extraction, and classification at scale |
| 4 | Workpaper preparation | EY Canvas + AI | AI auto-populates workpapers from client data; auto-generates testing summaries |
| 5 | Substantive testing | EY Helix + AI | Analytic procedures automated; AI-driven anomaly detection across full populations (not samples) |
| 6 | IFRS/compliance tracking | Atlas + AI | Natural language queries via EYQ; automated tracking of standard changes |
| 7 | Review | AI pre-review + human oversight | AI flags exceptions and drafts findings; senior reviews AI-flagged items rather than everything |
| 8 | Report issuance | GenAI drafting | AI generates first drafts of audit opinion and management letter |

**Key positioning:**
- "Human-led, technology-enabled workflows, enabled by intelligent agents that support the execution of defined tasks"
- "Reduce administrative burden for clients, improve risk assessments, and maintain the fundamental role of human judgment, skepticism and insight"

**Case study reference:** Grant Thornton + DataSnipper (independently verified): 600 → 30 hours across 2,400+ documents; 50-70% efficiency gain on key audit tasks.

## 5. Workflow Change Summary: Traditional vs. AI-Implemented

| Phase | Traditional | PwC AI | EY AI | Deloitte AI |
|-------|------------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Planning | Manual risk assessment + planning memos | AI benchmarks risk from prior data | AI auto-generates audit plan | AI suggests risk areas |
| Data Collection | Manual PBC list; PDF downloads | Intelligent agents orchestrate PBC workflows | Automated ingestion + AI validation | Omnia auto-ingest + DataDoc |
| Document Vouching | PDF-to-Excel; print/highlight/tick | Digital Workmate extracts structured data | Multi-agent AI handles evidence collection | Argus reads thousands of docs at once |
| Workpaper Prep | Manual population | Auto-populate from trial balance | Canvas + AI auto-populate | Omnia GenAI drafts workpapers |
| Testing | Manual sampling + execution | AI-assisted sampling; anomaly detection | AI analytic procedures in Helix | ControlCatalyst.AI automates SOX testing |
| IFRS Tracking | Manual Viewpoint research + spreadsheets | ChatPwC + auto IFRS change alerts | Atlas + AI; EYQ research | DART + AI; auto disclosure tracking |
| Review | Partner reads everything | AI pre-drafts; partner reviews output | AI flags exceptions; partner reviews flagged items | AI pre-drafts; partner review |
| Reporting | Manual drafting | GenAI drafts opinion + management letter | GenAI drafts communications | GenAI drafts memos + reports |

**Aggregate efficiency claims:**
- **DataSnipper / Grant Thornton benchmark:** 600 → 30 hours across 2,400+ documents (50-70% efficiency gain on key tasks)
- **EY:** 1.4 trillion journal lines processed; 2,000+ Indonesia professionals enabled
- **PwC:** End-to-end AI audit automation expected by 2026
- **Deloitte:** 600+ processes automated via UiPath; 4M labor-hours saved (global)
