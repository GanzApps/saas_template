## 1. ACCOUNTING & OUTSOURCING

### 1.1 Traditional Workflow by Firm

**PwC Indonesia**
PwC Indonesia offers Finance and Accounting Managed Services covering I2P, O2C, R2R, Tax Compliance, Fixed Asset, and Payroll.
1. Document intake (invoice, PO, bank statement, e-Faktur)
2. Manual data capture and entry into accounting system
3. 3-way match (PO vs. invoice vs. GRN)
4. Exception handling and approval routing
5. Journal entry preparation and posting
6. Reconciliation (bank, intercompany, tax)
7. Month-end close, accruals, deferrals
8. Financial statement package preparation
9. Statutory and regulatory filings
10. Dashboard/management reporting

*Acceleration layer:* 24x7 Financial Reporting Compliance Test Factory (RPA-driven testing and data acquisition); Workiva partnership; Financial Statements Assistant.

**EY Indonesia**
1. Plan using risk assessment and prior-year data
2. Obtain and store source documentation
3. Execute fieldwork: tick-and-tie, substantive testing
4. Collect evidence and document conclusions
5. Review managerial sign-offs
6. Issue audit opinion and statutory filings
7. Post-close reporting and analytics

*Note:* EY Indonesia emphasizes traditional assurance competencies augmented with analytics automation rather than fully automated from day one.

**Deloitte Indonesia**
1. Transaction processing & sub-ledger posting across multiple entities
2. Intercompany reconciliation and eliminations
3. Accruals and deferrals journals
4. Fixed asset register maintenance and depreciation runs
5. Bank and balance sheet reconciliations
6. Tax provision and compliance calculations
7. Statutory consolidation under IFAS
8. Management/board reporting pack assembly
9. Audit support and regulatory submission

*Note:* Pre-automation, Deloitte’s R2R was close-cycle-heavy reviews.

## 2. AUDIT

### 2.1 Traditional Workflow by Firm

**PwC**
1. Engagement acceptance/planning in Aura + Halo
2. Client data collection via Connect portal; manual trial balance download
3. Document vouching: PDF-to-Excel, print/highlight/tick
4. Substantive testing: workpapers in Aura; sampling in Excel/IDEA

5. IFRS tracking via Atlas + manual spreadsheets
6. Review via Canvas comments + email
7. Report drafted in Word; manual formatting

**Deloitte**
1. Engagement acceptance/planning in Omnia + Halo
2. Client data via Omnia upload portals; manual transfers
3. Manual PDF review; Argus run per engagement with manual tuning
4. Workpapers in Omnia hub; Excel cross-referencing
5. Sampling in Excel; testing in Omnia
6. IFRS tracking via DART + manual impact analysis
7. Partner sign-off on PDF exports from Omnia
8. Opinion + management letter drafted manually

## 3. ESG / SUSTAINABILITY

### 3.1 Traditional Workflow by Firm

**PwC Indonesia**
1. Discovery and Scoping: materiality assessment, stakeholder interviews, regulatory alignment (POJK 51/2017, SEOJK 16/2021)
2. Data Collection: manual surveys, paper-driven site reporting, Excel spreadsheets
3. Framework Mapping: manual mapping to GRI 2021, POJK 51/2017, SEOJK 16/2021, SASB, IFRS S1/S2
4. Report Drafting: MS Word / PowerPoint; heavy manual narrative writing
5. QC and Assurance Prep: Partner review; limited automated validation
6. External Assurance: limited-assurance procedures with KAP sign-off

**EY Indonesia**
1. ESG Maturity + Gap Assessment (EY ESG Suite modules)
2. Regulatory Scoping (POJK, SEOJK, IDX, GRI, IFRS S1/S2, SASB, TCFD)
3. Data Collection: vendor surveys, EHS extraction; IBM Envizi or ERP connectors in larger engagements
4. Data Transformation: Excel-based KPI aggregation
5. Reporting: specialist decks aligned to SEOJK / GRI / IFRS S1/S2
6. Assurance: ISAE 3000 procedures; limited or moderate assurance

**Deloitte Indonesia**
1. Materiality and Regulatory Mapping (ESRS / GRI / IFRS S1/S2)
2. Baseline and Gap Assessment
3. Data Collection: spreadsheet extraction, site visits, EHS dumps; Oracle/SAP ERP feeds in some engagements
4. Metric Calculation: GHG Protocol, PCAF, turnover calculations
5. Reporting: integrated report, standalone Sustainability Report, IDX ESG Rating pack
6. Assurance and Continuous Improvement: third-party assurance with data trails

**ERM Indonesia**
1. Issue Identification and Regulation Radar (Global Regulations Radar)
2. Data Collection: expert-led EHS audits, environmental due diligence, satellite imagery, emission factors
3. Impact/Risk Assessment and Mapping: GRI, IFC PS, ILO, IDX, climate risk, PSPK 1 / PSPK 2
4. Strategy and Roadmap: decarbonisation pathways, just transition plans
5. Implementation Monitoring: annual ESG KPI and qualitative data monitoring
6. Assurance and Attestation: ISAE 3000; often with separate auditor
